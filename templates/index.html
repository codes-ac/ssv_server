    About Us

    Government Shiv Singh Verma Girls Higher Secondary School is chattisgarh state board affilated hindi medium co-ed school ,situated in centre of the city in Dhamtari.

    This school started in 1963.It is 85 kms away from district of c.g raipur.There are total 45 well qualified teachers in school.Classes running from 6th to 12th.Courses offered science, commerce, arts,home-science,Vocationa health care and IT.School has well furnished I.t lab, science lab,health care lab and the center of attraction is atal tinkering lab set up by niti aayog delhi.

    सरकारी शिव सिंह वर्मा गर्ल्स हायर सेकेंडरी स्कूल, धमतरी शहर के केंद्र में स्थित है, जो छत्तीसगढ़ राज्य बोर्ड द्वारा प्रमाणित है।

    यह स्कूल 1963 में शुरू हुआ था। यह cg raipur जिले से 85 किलोमीटर दूर है। स्कूल में कुल 45 अच्छी तरह से योग्य शिक्षक हैं। 6 से 12 वीं तक चलने वाले क्लासेस। विज्ञान, वाणिज्य, कला, गृह-विज्ञान, व्यावसायिक स्वास्थ्य देखभाल और IT लैब, साइंस लैब, हेल्थ केयर लैब को अच्छी तरह से सुसज्जित किया है और आकर्षण का केंद्र नीती अयोग डेल्ही द्वारा स्थापित अटल टिंकरिंग लैब है।

    Principal's Corner

    Mrs. Beenu Mathew

    Our aim to provide all necessary facilities to make our students skilled and sharp so that we enhance an ordinary government school into extra ordinary school . so this way we can full fill the dream of financially weak parents. Learning is a passionate and interactive process of progressive change from ignorance to knowledge. Providing a platform to children to explore and evolve is the best way of learning. An environment that is conducive to learning is one which allows space to express opinions, set up communication and build skills and resources. While the school is committed to building such an atmosphere, which promotes spiritual, moral, cultural, mental and physical development of pupils, it also believes that true learning is not complete without parental support to the same ideals and when the two join hands, the magic will unfold.


    "A journey of thousand miles begins with a small steps, Education gives wings to fly, Intelligence to reason and think A Character for integrity and righteousness"

    ATAL TINKERING LAB

    Atal tinkering lab of Shiv singh verma aadarsh government higher secondary school dhamtari is the first atal tinkering lab selected by niti aayog in 2017.Through the atal tinkering lab the children are becoming proficient in various technical skills.Students have created various new technology devices and have won awards at the state and distric level.Students are also becoming experts in 3- d printing and going to start selling various 3-d printed articles in coming days.

    Our Atal tinkering lab is setup by TECHB , bhilai chattisgarh based company. They are our technical partner. We appreciate their excellent uninterrupted work and backend support service.Their training helped our student to make such innovative ideas into reality and made them skillful.
